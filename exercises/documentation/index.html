IN PRAISE OF SHADOWS

Designed by Ira Baran



Ira’s project focused on the imagery of the text, her use of shadow imagery ties into Tanazaki’s interpretations of western and Japanese architecture. I chose a gif to portray the movements of the shadow through the scroll. The black and white and simplisitc font is meant to distract from the large amount of text. I chose to included both web and mobile version of her site. The fonts in use were Nunito Sans, I chose to highlight her use of the golden brown within the links.
